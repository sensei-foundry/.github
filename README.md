# Sensei Foundry

Sensei Foundry is a modern technology foundry where bold ideas are forged into scalable systems. We design, prototype, and ship digital products that empower people and transform industries across Africa and beyond — from AI-powered utilities to developer automation and open-source innovation.

---

## Vision
To build the next generation of digital products for Africa and the world — products that feel thoughtful, fast, and reliable.

---

## What We Do
- Research and prototype software solutions for developers and everyday users.
- Build internal tools that remove friction from shipping and scaling products.
- Collaborate with partners and open-source communities to accelerate impact.

- Automation over repetition  
- Execution over talk  
- Real impact over noise
